Welcome to TiE Nagpur

The Indus Entrepreneurs (TiE) was founded in 1992 in Silicon Valley by a group of successful entrepreneurs, corporate executives, and senior professionals with roots in the Indus region.

TiE Nagpur is a chapter of this global non-profit organization, dedicated to fostering entrepreneurship in the Nagpur region. We provide a platform for startups, entrepreneurs, and aspiring business leaders to connect, learn, and grow through mentorship, networking, education, funding, and incubation.
